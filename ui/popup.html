Introduction

To enable or disable an entire extension switch the toggle button in the top-right corner. You also can do the same on a per-site basis by using "Run on this site" checkbox in the "Common" tab on the left pane.

You can choose one of the predefined color schemes from the drop-down list on the right pane and use it off the shelf.

Apart from that you can adjust your chosen scheme as you wish. For this purpose you have all the settings in the tabs on the left pane. Any changes you would have made will be immediately applied but only on the popup so you can safely play with it.

To assign the settings of your preference to the current website press "Apply" button on the right pane.

If you want to set up current settings as a default settings for all websites press "Set as default" button on the right pane.
